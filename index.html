About Bella Vista

Our story:

At BELLA VISTA, our story begins not in a kitchen, but in a small village tucked away in the rolling hills of Tuscany, where Nonna Rosa would wake up every morning to knead fresh pasta by hand, using nothing but flour, eggs, and love. Inspired by generations of Italian tradition, our family brought those cherished recipes and values across the seas to create a place where food feels like home. Founded in 2015, BELLA VISTA isn’t just a restaurant – it’s a celebration of family, heritage, and the simple joy of a meal shared around the table.
